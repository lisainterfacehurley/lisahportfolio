ROSE OF SHARON BUSHES

If you live in zones 4-6, you need to winterize your Rose of Sharon bushes to give them needed protection for the winter.

  1. Throughout the fall, water the Rose of Sharon bushes for five minutes each week. They need the water to get through the winter.
  2. Stop watering the bushes when the ground freezes. They will not need to be watered again until spring.
  3. Do not prune the bushes in the fall.
  4. Place a heap of leaves, straw, or pine needles around the base or crown of each bush.

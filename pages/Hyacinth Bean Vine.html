HYACINTH BEAN VINES

Hyacinth Bean Vines are grown as annual ornamental plants. Hyacinth Bean Vines are great for producing seed. The seed pods can be harvested for next year’s plants.

  1. Once the flowers die away, the seed pods are more significant and are purple in color.
  2. Allow the seed pods to remain on the vines and dry out. The purple pods will become brown and crisp.
  3. After the pods are brown and crisp, pick the pods off the vines.
  4. Tear open the brown dried seedpods and harvest the beans. These beans are the seeds for next year’s new seedlings.
  5. Place these seeds in an envelope and store in the refrigerator until next spring.

Everything is Coming Up Cosmos!

Cosmos are annuals. Cosmos are among the easiest of flowers to save the seeds from. After a bloom is spent, the petals fall off, the center dries up and seeds fan out. Harvest the seeds. Store the seeds inside of an envelope. Keep the seeds in a cool, dry location such as a basement or an attached garage. This method of harvesting seeds can apply to any variety of Cosmos.

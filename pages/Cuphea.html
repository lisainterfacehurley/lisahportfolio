CUPHEA: Bat Faced is a popular variety

Cuphea are grown as annuals. If you have the space inside, you can bring it indoors for the winter.

  1. If you have it planted in the ground, dig it up and pot it in a container. Pinch back the stem tips.
  2. If you have it planted in a container, simply pinch back the stem tips.
  3. Keep the Cuphea moist and in a sunny location.
  4. Throughout the winter, you will need to pinch back the stem tips, to maintain a dense compact shape.
  5. It may continue to bloom all winter.
